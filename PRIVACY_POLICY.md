# Privacy Policy for PitPulse

**Effective Date:** November 13, 2025
**Last Updated:** November 13, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to PitPulse. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use the PitPulse mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you create a PitPulse account, we collect:
- Email address (required for account creation and login)
- Username (your chosen display name)
- Password (stored securely using industry-standard encryption)
- Profile information (optional: bio, profile picture)

### 2. User-Generated Content
When you interact with the App, we collect:
- Reviews and ratings you submit for venues and bands
- Comments and feedback on venues and bands
- Photos you upload for venues, bands, or your profile
- Your interaction history (likes, favorites, follows)

### 3. Location Information
With your explicit permission, we may collect:
- Your approximate location (to help discover nearby venues)
- Location data is only collected when you use location-based features
- You can disable location access at any time in your device settings

### 4. Usage Data
We automatically collect certain technical information:
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- App usage statistics (features used, session duration)
- Error logs and crash reports (to improve app stability)
- IP address (for security and analytics purposes)

### 5. Photos and Camera Access
If you grant permission:
- Camera access (to take photos for profiles and venues)
- Photo library access (to upload existing photos)
- We only access photos when you explicitly choose to upload them

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 1. Provide and Improve Our Services
- Create and manage your user account
- Display your reviews and ratings to other users
- Enable you to discover venues and bands
- Personalize your experience based on your preferences
- Improve app functionality and user experience

### 2. Communication
- Send you account-related notifications
- Respond to your support requests
- Notify you of app updates or new features
- Send important service announcements

### 3. Safety and Security
- Protect against fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Investigate and prevent violations

### 4. Analytics and Performance
- Analyze app usage patterns
- Monitor app performance and stability
- Identify and fix bugs
- Optimize user experience

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Information In These Limited Cases:

#### 1. Public Information
- Your username, profile picture, and reviews are publicly visible to all App users
- Other users can see your ratings and comments on venues and bands

#### 2. Service Providers
- We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App
- These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information
- Examples: cloud hosting providers, analytics services, customer support tools

#### 3. Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law:
- To comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To investigate fraud or security issues
- To respond to government requests

#### 4. Business Transfers
- If PitPulse is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred
- You will be notified of any such change in ownership

### We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Share your email address publicly
- ❌ Use your photos for purposes other than displaying them in the App
- ❌ Track your location without your explicit permission

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your information as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days
- **Legal Obligations:** Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal requirements
- **Backups:** Data in backup systems may persist for up to 90 days after deletion

## Your Privacy Rights

### You Have the Right To:

#### 1. Access Your Data
- View all personal information we have about you
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format

#### 2. Update Your Information
- Edit your profile information at any time
- Update your email address or username
- Change your password

#### 3. Delete Your Data
- Delete individual reviews or comments
- Request deletion of your entire account
- Contact us at support@pitpulse.app to request data deletion

#### 4. Control Location Access
- Enable or disable location services at any time
- Manage location permissions in your device settings

#### 5. Opt Out of Communications
- Unsubscribe from promotional emails
- Disable push notifications in app settings

#### 6. Data Portability
- Request a machine-readable copy of your data
- Transfer your data to another service

### To Exercise Your Rights:
- In-app settings for most privacy controls
- Email us at privacy@pitpulse.app for data requests
- We will respond to requests within 30 days

## Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement multiple safeguards:

### Security Measures:
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Secure Storage:** Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and penetration testing
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Data stored on secure cloud servers

### Your Responsibility:
- Keep your password confidential
- Use a strong, unique password
- Log out on shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Children's Privacy

PitPulse is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information:
- We will delete that information immediately
- We will terminate the account
- We will notify the user via email

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at support@pitpulse.app.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

### Your CCPA Rights:
- **Right to Know:** What personal information we collect, use, and share
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at privacy@pitpulse.app.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

### Your GDPR Rights:
- **Right to Access:** Obtain confirmation of data processing
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a structured format
- **Right to Object:** Object to certain data processing activities
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at privacy@pitpulse.app.

### Legal Basis for Processing:
- **Consent:** You have given explicit consent
- **Contract:** Processing is necessary to provide our services
- **Legal Obligation:** We must comply with legal requirements
- **Legitimate Interests:** Processing is necessary for our business operations
